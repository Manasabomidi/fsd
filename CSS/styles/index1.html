What does saving time mean?What is Daylight Saving Time (DST)?
What is Daylight Saving Time (DST)? Daylight Saving Time (DST) is the
practice of turning the clock ahead as warmer weather approaches and back
as it becomes colder again. The goal of Daylight Saving Time is to make
better use of daylight by prolonging the amount of time we can spend
outside during daylight hours
